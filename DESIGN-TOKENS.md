## Design Principles
1. **Medical trust**: Clean, precise, no flashy elements
2. **Mobile-first**: All layouts designed for 375px first
3. **Warm professional**: Copper accent on neutral base
4. **Generous whitespace**: Let content breathe
5. **Scandinavian minimalism**: Less is more
